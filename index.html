  • Barpeta Kendriya Vidyalaya shines in CBSE Class X exam 2020

    The results of CBSE Class X final exam, 2020 were declared on Wednesday. Barpeta Kendriya Vidyalaya recorded 100 per cent pass percentage. All total 36 candidates appeared and all of them were able to score satisfactory marks. Among the, 12 students secured above 90 per cent, 18 above 80 per cent, 2 above 70 per cent, 2 above 60 per cent and 2 above 50 per cent. Jyotisman Pathak, son of Rajiv Pathak and Karabi Pathak, secured the highest percentage with 97.6 per cent in the school.

International Mathematics Olympiad Work Book -Class 5

International Mathematics Olympiad Work Book -Class 5

International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO) Workbooks are designed to familiarize students with the type of questions coming in Olympiad exams. The Workbook contains chapter-wise multiple choice question (MCQs) Bank divided in the section of Logical Reasoning, Mathematical Reasoning, Everyday Mathematics and Achievers Section, followed by Hints and explanation in the end of the book. The IMO Olympiad workbook also contains latest 2020 Olympiad exam paper with hints answer to give recent IMO Olympiad exam understanding. A bubble (OMR) sheet is also given with exercises, so that students can practice Marking answers. It gives complete syllabus question practice and quick recap with self-analysis for great performance in the Olympiads. Grandma's Bag of Stories

Grandma's Bag of Stories

Memories of a grandparent spinning tales around animals and mysterious characters have kept many of us rapt till date. Sudha Murty's Grandma’s Bag of Stories is simply delightful. The story starts with Anand, Krishna, Raghu and Meena arriving at their grandparents’ house in Shiggaon. Overjoyed Ajji and Ajja(Grandmother and grandfather in Kannada) get the house ready, while Ajji prepares delicious snacks for children. Finally, times comes when everyone gathers around Ajji, as she opens her big bag of stories. She tells stories of kings and cheats, princesses and onions, monkeys and mice and scorpions and hidden treasures.

Though unlikely in combination, stories makes perfect sense when Grandma is the one narrating them. This book is ideal for young children and those who are 5+ in age. Stories are accompanied by colourful illustrations and morals. Lucid and simple language of the book, makes reading a pleasure.
